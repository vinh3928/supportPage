SnapIt Support

Support

SnapIt helps you capture text with the camera, stitch OCR results together, and manage a running transcript on iPhone.

Contact

For support, billing, or bug reports, email support@example.com.

Common Issues

  • If camera access is missing, enable it in iPhone Settings for SnapIt.
  • If copy is locked, confirm your subscription is active and try Restore Purchases.
  • If OCR misses text, retake the photo with better lighting and less motion blur.
  • If stitched text repeats, clear the transcript and rescan from the correct position.

Subscription Support

Copying stitched text may require an active monthly subscription. Purchases and billing are managed by Apple through your App Store account.
