SnapIt Privacy Policy

SnapIt is an iPhone app that uses the camera to capture text, performs OCR to extract words from images, stitches scanned text into a transcript, and optionally lets subscribed users copy that text to the device clipboard.

Information We Collect

SnapIt is designed to minimize data collection.

  • Camera images you choose to capture inside the app.
  • Text extracted from those images using on-device OCR.
  • Subscription and purchase status provided by Apple for copy access.

How Information Is Used

  • To scan and recognize text from camera images.
  • To stitch recognized text into a continuous transcript.
  • To determine whether the Copy feature is unlocked through an active subscription.
  • To copy the stitched transcript to your device clipboard when you choose to do so.

On-Device Processing

SnapIt processes OCR text locally on your device using Apple frameworks. We do not intentionally upload your scanned images or recognized text to our servers.

Clipboard Use

If you use the Copy feature, SnapIt places the stitched transcript on your device clipboard so that you can paste it into another app.

Subscriptions and Payments

Subscription purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment card information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not intentionally share your scanned images or recognized text with third parties except as required to provide Apple in-app purchase functionality or comply with law.

Data Retention

Recognized text and stitched transcript content remain on your device unless you delete or clear them. If future versions of SnapIt add cloud features, this policy will be updated.

Your Choices

  • You can deny camera permission in iPhone Settings.
  • You can clear the stitched transcript inside the app.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID subscription settings.

Children's Privacy

SnapIt is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
